Una Notte A North Beach

2018

Una Notte A North Beach

Una Notte a North Beach is a collection of free instrumental songs available via Josh's YouTube page, for artists and creators to use and help inspire their own works. The songs set scenes inspired by locations in the San Francisco Italian neighborhood North Beach.


Blending the sounds of 70s euro crime soundtracks with San Francisco psych rock, Una Notte a North Beach creates a soundtrack to a movie that hasn't been made, but is lived every night on the streets of North Beach.

About

Josh Lippi is an American multi-instrumentalist, songwriter, producer from Sacramento, CA. He currently is the touring bassist/guitarist for K.Flay and skateboard legend and musician Tommy Guerrero . His studio credits include recordings for Logic, Ray Barbee, Alice Russell & more. He is a group member of The Park, Blktop Project and Rock & Roll band Lee Bob & the Truth.
